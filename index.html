How Rainwater Co-op Works

We combine the purchasing power of independent companies to negotiate pricing that was previously only available to large-scale operations.

Join the Waitlist

Sign up with your company details to reserve your spot. There are no membership fees—the co-op will be funded through a small percentage of the negotiated savings.

We Aggregate Volume

Your orders will be combined with other member companies, giving us the collective volume to qualify for pricing tiers usually reserved for national distributors.

Save an Est. 10–15%

Members will receive discounted pricing on tanks, filtration systems, piping, pumps, and more. You order directly from vendors—we just unlock the better price.

Built by people in the industry, for people in the industry.

Rainwater Co-op was founded by a group of independent rainwater harvesting professionals who were tired of watching larger competitors get better pricing simply because of order volume.

We believe that small and mid-size companies deserve the same access to competitive supply pricing. The cooperative model makes that possible—without giving up your independence.
