About Rainwater Co-op

Who we are, why we started, and how we help independent companies save.

Our Story

Rainwater Co-op was founded in 2019 by a small group of independent rainwater collection professionals in central Texas. They shared a common frustration: national competitors with higher order volumes consistently received better supply pricing—not because they were better companies, but simply because they ordered more.

The solution was straightforward. By pooling their purchasing volume, independent companies could qualify for the same bulk pricing tiers as the largest operations in the industry. That idea became Rainwater Co-op.

Today, we represent over 120 member companies across 22 states, and our collective purchasing power has saved members more than $2.4 million in supply costs.

Our Mission & Values

Everything we do is guided by a few core principles.

Fair Access

Every company in our cooperative, regardless of size, gets access to the same negotiated pricing. Volume discounts shouldn't only be available to the biggest players.

Independence First

You keep running your business your way. We never require exclusivity or lock-in. The co-op exists to serve its members, not the other way around.

Transparency

Our financials, vendor agreements, and pricing tiers are open to all members. You always know exactly how savings are calculated and where your money goes.
