Why Rainwater Partners Exists

This isn't about changing how you run your business. It's about giving you the buying power to compete—and win.

The industry is growing. Your margins shouldn't shrink.

Rainwater harvesting is about to experience explosive growth. New regulations, water scarcity, and growing consumer demand are creating massive opportunity for independent providers.

But here's the problem: larger competitors have always had access to better pricing simply because of order volume. Not because they're better companies. Not because they deliver better work. Just because they buy more.

Rainwater Partners was started to fix that. By aggregating the purchasing volume of independent providers, we give every member the same access to preferred pricing, freight advantages, and priority supplier relationships that used to be reserved for the biggest players.

How We Operate

Independence First

You keep your brand, your customers, and your business decisions. We never require exclusivity. You choose what and when to buy.

Full Transparency

Clear pricing. Negotiated terms you can see. No hidden fees or complicated structures. Our model only works when you're saving money.

Aligned Incentives

We succeed when you save. No upfront fees. The model is designed so that every dollar we earn comes from value we've already delivered.
