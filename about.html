About

Why Rainwater Partners exists

Independent rainwater providers often purchase the same equipment as larger players—but without access to the same pricing and supplier terms.

Rainwater Partners brings together purchasing demand across independent providers to help secure better pricing and more efficient supplier relationships.

The goal is simple:

Make it easier to access better economics—without changing how you run your business.
