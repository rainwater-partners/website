Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about how Rainwater Co-op works and what to expect as a member.

Rainwater Co-op is a purchasing cooperative made up of independent rainwater collection and harvesting companies. By combining our members' purchasing volume, we negotiate bulk pricing from vendors and manufacturers—discounts that individual companies typically can't access on their own. Think of it as a buying group specifically for the rainwater industry.
There are no upfront membership fees. The cooperative is sustained through a small service percentage applied to orders placed through our negotiated vendor agreements. This percentage is always less than the discount you receive, so you save money from day one. Full details on the fee structure are provided during onboarding.
Most members see savings of 10–15% on the supplies they purchase through the cooperative. The exact amount depends on what you're buying and from which vendors. When you sign up, we'll provide a personalized estimate based on your current spending and the vendors you work with.
We currently have negotiated pricing agreements with over 45 vendors and manufacturers covering tanks, filtration systems, pumps, piping, first-flush diverters, UV treatment systems, and more. We're continuously expanding our vendor network based on member requests. During sign-up, you can tell us which vendors you'd like to see added and we'll prioritize accordingly.
No. There are no exclusivity requirements. You can continue to purchase from any vendor or distributor you currently work with. The co-op is simply an additional channel that gives you access to better pricing when you want it. Use it for all of your orders or just the ones where the savings make sense.
You order directly from our partner vendors using your co-op member account. The discounted pricing is automatically applied. Products ship directly to you from the vendor—we don't warehouse or redistribute anything. It works just like your current ordering process, but at a lower price.
No. The cooperative is designed specifically to help smaller and mid-size companies access the same pricing as larger operations. Whether you spend $10,000 or $500,000 annually on supplies, you're welcome to join and will receive the same negotiated pricing as every other member.
Yes. There are no long-term contracts or lock-in periods. If you decide the co-op isn't a fit, you can leave at any time with no penalties. We believe the value should speak for itself.
We currently have members in 22 states across the U.S. Most of our vendor agreements are national, so as long as the vendor ships to your area, you can take advantage of the pricing regardless of where your company is located. We're actively looking to expand our reach.
Fill out our sign-up form with some basic information about your company and the vendors you work with. We'll follow up within 1–2 business days with a personalized savings estimate and details on how membership works. There's no commitment required just to learn more.
