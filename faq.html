Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about how Rainwater Co-op works and what to expect as a member.

Rainwater Co-op is a purchasing cooperative for independent rainwater collection and harvesting companies, launching in September 2026. By combining our members' purchasing volume, we'll negotiate bulk pricing from suppliers and manufacturers—discounts that individual companies typically can't access on their own. Think of it as a buying group specifically for the rainwater industry.
There are no upfront membership fees. The cooperative is sustained through a small service percentage applied to orders placed through our negotiated vendor agreements. This percentage is always less than the discount you receive, so you save money from day one. Full details on the fee structure are provided during onboarding.
We estimate members will see an additional 10–15% in savings on the supplies they purchase through the cooperative. The exact amount will depend on what you're buying and from which suppliers. When you join the waitlist, we'll provide a personalized estimate based on your current spending and the suppliers you work with.
We currently have 30+ supplier partners lined up, covering tanks, filtration systems, pumps, piping, first-flush diverters, UV treatment systems, and more. We're continuously expanding our supplier network based on member requests. When you join the waitlist, you can tell us which suppliers you'd like to see added and we'll prioritize accordingly.
No. There are no exclusivity requirements. You can continue to purchase from any vendor or distributor you currently work with. The co-op is simply an additional channel that gives you access to better pricing when you want it. Use it for all of your orders or just the ones where the savings make sense.
You'll order directly from our partner suppliers using your co-op member account. The discounted pricing will be automatically applied. Products ship directly to you from the supplier—we don't warehouse or redistribute anything. It works just like your current ordering process, but at a lower price.
No. The cooperative is designed specifically to help smaller and mid-size companies access the same pricing as larger operations. Whether you spend $10,000 or $500,000 annually on supplies, you're welcome to join and will receive the same negotiated pricing as every other member.
Yes. There are no long-term contracts or lock-in periods. If you decide the co-op isn't a fit, you can leave at any time with no penalties. We believe the value should speak for itself.
We already have 75+ companies on the waitlist across the U.S. Most of our supplier agreements will be national, so as long as the supplier ships to your area, you'll be able to take advantage of the pricing regardless of where your company is located.
Join the waitlist with some basic information about your company and the suppliers you work with. You'll receive a confirmation email right away, and we'll follow up with a personalized savings estimate and details on how membership will work when we launch in September 2026. There's no commitment required.
